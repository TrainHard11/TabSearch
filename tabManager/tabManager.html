Tab Management

Press **1-9** to move the **current active tab** to that specific position in the window.
Press **H** to move the current tab one position to the left. (Popup stays open)
Press **L** to move the current tab one position to the right. (Popup stays open)
Use **Up/Down Arrows (or J/K)** to select a window below (including "New Empty Window"). Press **Enter** to move the current tab to the selected window.
